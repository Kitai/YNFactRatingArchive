In this experiment you will read short dialogues between two people in the form of a question and an answer. You will then be asked to rate to what extent the answer sounds natural to you in light of the question, by choosing a value between "completely unnatural' (1) to "completely natural" (7). There is not a right or a wrong answer. Simply make the choice based on how well you feel the answer works for the preceding question. For example, in the following case, it would seem like the question is straightforwardly answered:



Question: Is John travelling to Paris next month?
Answer: No, he isn’t travelling to Paris next month.

But in the next example, the answer seems to be rather odd:



Question: Is John travelling to Paris next month?
Answer: Yes, although he isn’t travelling to Paris next month.

So you might choose 7 and 1 for these respectively.
